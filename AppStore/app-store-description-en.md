## Description

BVC Hälsokort is a digital version of the Swedish child health card ("Barnets hälsokort") — the booklet every parent in Sweden receives from the child health center (BVC).

Now you can keep it in your pocket as a handy complement to the paper card.

───

FEATURES

◆ Personal Details
Save your child's name, birth date, personal number, and other key information.

◆ Growth Tracking
Record weight, length, and head circumference at every checkup. Watch your child's growth over time.

◆ Growth Charts
Your child's measurements displayed on clear charts with WHO reference curves — separate curves for boys and girls.

◆ Development
Follow your child's developmental milestones and note important moments.

◆ Vaccinations
Keep track of all vaccinations with dates and types. Lockable entries prevent accidental edits to confirmed records.

◆ Multiple Children
Full support for multiple children in the same app — perfect for families with siblings.

───

YOUR PRIVACY COMES FIRST

All data is stored exclusively on your device. Nothing is sent to the cloud, no accounts are needed, no tracking, no ads, no analytics. Your child's health information stays with you — and only you.
