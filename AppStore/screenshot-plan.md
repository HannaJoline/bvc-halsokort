# Screenshot Plan — BVC Hälsokort

Target: 6 screenshots, iPhone 6.7" (1290×2796 px).
Each screenshot has a caption overlay at the top (~20% of screen) with the app UI below.

---

## Screenshot 1 — Hero / Child Overview
**Screen:** Main screen showing a child's profile card (name, age, photo placeholder)
**Demo data:** "Alma Lindqvist", born 2024-03-15
**Caption (SV):** "Barnets hälsokort — alltid med dig"
**Caption (EN):** "Your child's health card — always with you"

## Screenshot 2 — Growth Tracking
**Screen:** Growth entry list showing several recorded measurements
**Demo data:** 4–5 entries from birth to 12 months with weight, length, head circumference
**Caption (SV):** "Följ tillväxten steg för steg"
**Caption (EN):** "Track growth step by step"

## Screenshot 3 — Growth Chart
**Screen:** Growth chart with WHO reference curves and the child's plotted data points
**Demo data:** Weight curve for a girl, 0–12 months, points following ~50th percentile
**Caption (SV):** "Tillväxtkurvor med WHO-referens"
**Caption (EN):** "Growth charts with WHO reference"

## Screenshot 4 — Vaccinations
**Screen:** Vaccination list showing completed and upcoming vaccinations
**Demo data:** 3–4 completed vaccines (BCG, Rotavirus, etc.) with dates, 1–2 upcoming
**Caption (SV):** "Alla vaccinationer på ett ställe"
**Caption (EN):** "All vaccinations in one place"

## Screenshot 5 — Development Milestones
**Screen:** Development/milestones view with checkmarks and notes
**Demo data:** A few milestones checked off (first smile, sits up, first word)
**Caption (SV):** "Följ utvecklingen och milstolparna"
**Caption (EN):** "Follow milestones and development"

## Screenshot 6 — Privacy / Multiple Children
**Screen:** Child selector showing 2 children, with a privacy badge or lock icon visible
**Demo data:** "Alma" and "Erik" as two children
**Caption (SV):** "Flera barn. All data stannar på din enhet."
**Caption (EN):** "Multiple children. All data stays on your device."

---

## Design Notes
- Use a consistent background color behind captions (match icon palette: soft blue/teal)
- White caption text, bold, ~48pt equivalent
- Rounded device frame mockup optional but recommended
- Keep demo data realistic — Swedish names, Swedish vaccine schedule
- Light mode UI preferred for screenshots
